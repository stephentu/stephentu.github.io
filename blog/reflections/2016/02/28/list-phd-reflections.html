List of PhD reflections

28 Feb 2016 on reflections

Over the past few years, several computer science PhD graduates have taken time to write about their personal experience during graduate school. Here is an incomplete list of these reflections I could find on the internet. Of course, I am definitely missing some, so let me know if yours is missing, and I will add it. The links below are organized by date.

  • Lindsey Kuper - What the first half of grad school was like. (2011)
  • Evan Jones - Farewell to MIT. (2011)
  • Lindsey Kuper - What the second half of grad school will be like. (2012)
  • Philip Guo - The Ph.D. Grind. (2012)
  • Adam Marcus - The N=1 guide to grad school. (2013)
  • Chris Martens - How to get a Ph.D. in computer science if you're me. (2015)
  • Peter Bailis - I Loved Graduate School. (2016)
  • Jean Yang - What My PhD Was Like. (2016)

Interestingly enough, the list of students skews heavily towards systems and programming language researchers. Of course, this is probably a selection bias. Having said that, it would be interesting to hear from the TCS side!
