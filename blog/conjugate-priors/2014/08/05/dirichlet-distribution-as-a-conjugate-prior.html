The Dirichlet distribution as a conjugate prior for multinomial/categorical likelihood models

05 Aug 2014 on conjugate-priors

Here's a quick writeup on both the Dirichlet-Multinomial and the Dirichlet-Categorical likelihood models, which are often used to model text data. These two models are often confused for one another, and furthermore I could not find any easy to access references online which gather all the relevant predictive/posterior/marginal distributions for the models. Enjoy!
